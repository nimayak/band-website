Cillian/Vera/Nate

Band Members

We are a trio of young musicians, based in Melbourne and we love indie pop music. We met back in 1999, in highschool and we were always keen to break into the indie music scene.
With our meaningful song lyrics, catchy beats and flashy perfomances we hope to wow our fans.

Cillian Snow

Drum Player.
Birthday: 6.01.1988
Favourite Bands: Passion Pit, Blink-182
Cillian grew up in Sunshine Coast, Australia and has always loved music. Growing up in a musical household, with a mother and a father who were music teachers, music ran in his blood. He picked up the guitar at age six and was always seen serenading his family and friends whenever he could get the chance. He is known to be very serious at times but is fun-loving at heart.

Vera Mode

Vocals.
Birthday: 28.10.1989
Favourite Bands: Panic! At The Disco, Fun., mewithoutYOU
Vera was a hyperactive bubbly child who loved being in the spotlight. She was introduced to rock music by her father when she was eight and she's been singing and songwriting ever since. She grew up in Mebourne, Australia and at a young age, she realised peforming was her true calling.

Nate Lightly

Guitarist.
Birthday: 17.3.1988
Favourite Bands: Death Cab for Cutie, Paramore
Growing up in Cairnes, Australia, sport had always been Nate's passion but when he got into an accident and was unable to move for six months, he had to find another way to pass his time. He taught himself the guitar and fell in love with the artform. Nate has always been a very passionate, hard-working guy who never gave up no matter the circumstance.

Gallery of Moments

Over the years, we've performed all over Australia and we've taken some photos to eternalise the memories. We've been lucky enough to open for a variety of bands such as Mumford and Sons and Paramore! Words couldn't describe the pure elation we felt just to be in the estatic concert environment. These photos depict concerts and festivals held at some provincial towns in Victoria and some of our own hometowns!

We'd like to thank you; the concertgoers, the die-hard fans and the casual music downloaders for displaying your love! Without your support and devotion we wouldn't be able to share our love of music and songwriting with others. We make music for you.
